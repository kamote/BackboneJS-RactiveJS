About Us

Ubook is a FREE and the first service in Norway that allows you to easily search and book appointment with the nearest hair or beauty salon.

If you own a hair or beauty salon, you can register your salon or all of your branches on Ubook to start accepting customers online.

Your business will be more searchable and accessible to your customers, you can increase sales, decrease no shows and you can acquire new customers. It’s a unique and complete service for you to easily accept booking and manage your business online.

People go to “Finn.no” if they need an apartment, if they want to know more about something they search on Google and we believe that Ubook will be the first place people will visit to find hair and beauty salons.

Register your hair or beauty salon on Ubook to get more customers with Online Booking.
